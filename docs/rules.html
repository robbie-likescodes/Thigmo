🌸 Thigmo Rules — Botanical Battlefield

Welcome to Thigmo, where rival floral lineages wage a slow, intelligent war for dominance of living soil. Every stem, tendril, and rootlet competes for one destiny: become the reigning invasive species.

This isn’t random growth. This is botanical strategy.

1) Objective: Rule the Soil

Your goal is to overrun your rival’s bloom network through positional pressure and oxygen starvation.

  • You win immediately when you have captured 10 enemy blooms.
  • Captures happen through tactical encirclement and deprivation of growth space (liberties).

2) Components of the Living Battlefield

8 terrain tiles (movable soil patches) make the board.

Two bloom factions:

PurpleOrange

A vertical growth column rises on each tile (stacks of blooms upward).

3) Board Ecology: How Space Exists in Thigmo

  1. The world is an infinite coordinate field.
  2. Only 8 soil tiles exist in the match.
  3. No two tiles may occupy the same coordinate.
  4. Blooms occupy vertical positions above each tile: z = 0, 1, 2...
  5. If a tile moves, the entire bloom stack on that tile moves with it.

4) Turn Cycle: Pulse of Growth

Phase A — Shift One Soil Tile (Required)

  • Move exactly one influenced tile by one step (orthogonal or diagonal).
  • Destination must be empty.
  • All 8 tiles must remain orthogonally connected after moving.

Phase B — Plant One Bloom (Required unless full)

  • Place one bloom of your color onto any tile.
  • It lands on the top of that stack.
  • You cannot place if the stack is already at the 7-bloom max.

Phase C — Resolve Withering (Captures)

  • Opponent zero-liberty groups are removed first.
  • Then your own zero-liberty groups are removed.
  • Repeat until no new removals occur.

5) Tile Influence

A soil tile is movable if your bloom is on that tile or on any neighboring tile (8-direction neighborhood).

6) Groups and Liberties

Groups connect by orthogonal 3D adjacency only. A liberty is an empty orthogonally adjacent cell where growth could exist.

7) Win Condition

You win instantly when your captured enemy blooms reaches 10 after resolution.
